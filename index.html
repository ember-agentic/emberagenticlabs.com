Ember Agentic Labs

Human creativity, amplified.

A studio for agentic AI systems applied to real-world domains. Tools where AI agents do real work on real problems — with structure, memory, and quality gates that compound across sessions instead of evaporating with the chat window.

What we're building

A substrate that compounds.

The bottleneck for AI in real work isn't the model — it's context. What's been decided, why, against which constraint, with which trade-off accepted. Most tools throw all of that away at the end of every chat.

Ember's bet: a structured substrate that grows with the work — artifacts, decisions, and history as linked first-class entities — is what lets AI work productively at the scale humans actually build at.

Crucible proved it under load — built using itself. Read the story →
